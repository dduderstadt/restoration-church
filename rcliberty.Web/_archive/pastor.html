OUR PASTOR

JP Stafford, Lead Pastor

JP Stafford is the founding and lead pastor of Restoration Church. JP is a gifted communicator and teacher, convicted about speaking the truth as plainly as possible. God has given him a vision and a passion for helping people meet Jesus, and each week he shares God’s word and its practical application in our daily lives.

JP, his wife Heather, their son Chase and their daughter Karli live in Liberty, Missouri. You can read all of JP’s unfiltered thoughts about life and leadership on his twitter page: @JohnPStafford
