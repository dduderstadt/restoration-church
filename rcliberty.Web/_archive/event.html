Duck Dynasty Funny Man to Visit Local Church
and Host Community Event

Liberty, Mo. (September 10, 2015) — We’ve heard the Duck Dynasty crew’s values of “faith, family and ducks” on their popular reality television show, so it shouldn’t come as a surprise that one of their own extended some southern hospitality to a small, Liberty church. Justin Martin – called “Martin” by his Robertson family friends – will be speaking at Restoration Church and hosting the Family Dynasty Day event on Sunday, October 11.


It all started when J.P. Stafford, pastor of Restoration Church, reached out to Martin – via Twitter – inviting him to speak at the church. The church recently opened a new location and Stafford hoped a visit from Martin would bring new visitors. The Duck Dynasty crew is on a tight schedule and booked through the year, but Martin felt drawn to help out this local church and agreed to make the trip.


Stafford said he hoped for a miracle, but was still shocked by Martin’s response. “I just felt like Martin was the person I needed to reach out to,” said Stafford, “but I was still surprised by his overwhelming kindness and generosity.” In addition to agreeing to speak at the church, Martin arranged to participate in an event after the service. “Restoration Church is excited to celebrate Martin’s appearance with the entire community,” said Stafford. “The Family Dynasty Day event will be a great way for families to spend time together and meet Martin.”


Martin will begin speaking at 10:30 a.m. at Restoration Church (1908 Plumbers Way - Suite 400 - Liberty, Missouri 64068). The Family Dynasty Day event will begin immediately after and will conclude at 2 p.m. The event will include a petting zoo, fire truck, police car, multiple inflatables, face painting, food, drinks and more. Martin will be available to sign autographs, but attendees must bring an item for him to sign. For more event details, please visit: www.rcliberty.com.
The Family Dynasty Day sponsors include Professional Ag Services Inc., LLT Inc. and Open Mic Inc.

Event information

Date: Sunday, October 11, 2015
